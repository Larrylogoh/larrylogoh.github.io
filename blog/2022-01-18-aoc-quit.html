I quit Advent of Code 2021

2022-01-08

I did it for good reasons this time I promise.

The main reason I’m quitting is I really do not have the time. The way I see it, I don’t have the skills to solve the puzzles as quickly as I would like. It takes me way too long to solve the problems and I assume the complexity will continue to increase at least to a certain point.

I have work(National Service) now, which takes around 12 hours of my time. I also have side projects(coming soon) that will take up most of the extra time. So I’ll give up on last year’s advent of code. By the time this year ends, I should be able to handle the next one.
